About
Company
Established in 2017, Shree Balaji Industries is a leading manufacturer of concrete blocks
and pavement tiles situated in Jalandhar, Punjab.

We manufacture top quality concrete block tiles, Bricks, Blocks of standard size. Also we deliver the customized size products as per the requirement. We take complete care to ensure top quality of each product.

Apart from beauty, we focus on the durability of the product. Concrete blocks not only provide a better weather proof construction, but also save time and construction cost compared to the traditional clay brick construction.
